About Me

I started programming back in 8th grade (2009), editing game files in C++ in order to start a business providing a service to gamers playing Call of Duty MW2 on the XBOX 360. Unfortunately, I didn't know web development at the time so I couldn't build out an e-commerce website to automate the purchase and payment.

Fast forward to today, I've worked for a tech consulting firm doing mostly DevOps with IBM Cloud and IBM mainframes. I've also operated my own Amazon Seller business (started at April 2020 during start of COVID lockdown) and assisted other companies with their Amazon Seller businesses.

While I love being an engineer and enjoy the ability to create things, Amazon was a very eye opening experience. I was quickly forced to learn more to be successful on Amazon including everything from Marketing and Campaigns, Branding, Product Development and Research, Supply Chain and Inventory Management, just to name a few.

Recently, I've taken up a bigger interest in Data Science. While operating my own Amazon business, I realized the power of data analysis to optimize margins, advertising costs, and improve internal processes and generally, to make more informed business decisions. However, I want to take this a step further and learn more complex ML models for analysis and for development.
